OpenLexington is a non-profit, non-partisan organization that is working to promote local government transparency and empower citizens through open data.

OpenLexington Volunteers are citizen engineers, professional software developers, analysts, artists, designers, students, and concerned residents. You can contribute too. Lend your talents to build something meaningful and useful. Get involved with us.
